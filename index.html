What Exactly Do We Do?

.

Sunset Drive is an Outdoor Movie Theatre. We have been thrilling audiences with some of Hollywood's biggest movies.

Outdoor Entertainment

Our brand new digital projection system is state-of-the-art and produces the sharpest, brightest pictures. The better picture combined with our powerful surround surround subwofer create a magical atmosphere. It’s an experience so real, you’ll feel like you’re right in the middle of the action.

Choose From A Wide Selection of Blockbuster Movies

Select your choice of movies from prominent Hollywood and independent studios and distributors from around the world. With over 13,000 titles available, we offer a wide range of non-theatrical licenses for film screenings and viewings outside the home or cinema.

Fun Games & Outdoor Activities

Because of the variety of fun activities you can participate in, there's never a dull moment at any of our screenings. Arrive early and play table tennis, pick out your choice from numerous board games or challenge your friends to a Karaoke match. Get in the right mood before the start of your movie!
